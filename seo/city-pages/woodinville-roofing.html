Woodinville Roofing Questions

How much does a roof replacement cost in Woodinville?

Most Woodinville homes run between $12,000 and $25,000 depending on the size of the roof, how many layers we're tearing off, and whether there's rot in the deck. Cedar shake tear-offs cost more because the material is heavier and takes longer to remove. I give exact numbers in the estimate, not ranges.

My roof has thick green moss on it. Do I need a full replacement?

Not always. If the shingles underneath are still in good shape, we can treat the moss, clean the roof, and add zinc strips to slow regrowth. But if the moss has been there for years and lifted the shingle edges, water has been getting under them. At that point, treatment won't fix the damage already done. I'll tell you honestly which situation you're in.

We get a lot of wind off the valley. Will new shingles hold up?

GAF HDZ shingles are rated for 130 mph winds when installed with the full system. That's more than Woodinville throws at them. The key is proper nailing and sealing, which comes down to the crew doing the work. Our installers are GAF-certified, and every job gets a final inspection before we call it done.

How long does a Woodinville roof replacement take?

A standard single-family home takes 2 to 4 days. Steeper roofs, second stories, and cedar shake tear-offs add time. We don't leave a roof open overnight. If we strip a section, we seal it before we leave for the day, rain or not.
