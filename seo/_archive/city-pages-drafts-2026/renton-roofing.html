Renton Roofing Questions

My Renton home was built in the 1960s. Is the roof deck likely rotted?

Homes from that era used 1x6 skip sheathing or thin plywood, and after 60 years of rain, some of it will have soft spots. We check the deck during every tear-off and replace damaged sections before installing new shingles. The estimate includes a per-sheet price for plywood replacement so there are no surprises if we find rot.

What's the difference between your flat roof options?

EverGuard PVC is a membrane system. We heat-weld the seams, which creates a watertight bond stronger than the membrane itself. Uniflex Silicone is a coating system we apply over existing flat roofs that are still structurally sound. PVC is the full replacement. Silicone is the restoration option. I'll tell you which one fits your situation.

How much does a roof cost in Renton?

A typical Renton home runs $10,000 to $22,000 for a full replacement. The range depends on square footage, roof pitch, number of layers coming off, and how much deck repair we find underneath. Flat roof sections cost more per square foot than sloped. I give you the exact number in the estimate, broken out by line item.

Can you match the look of my neighbor's roof? We're in an HOA.

GAF makes their shingles in dozens of colors across three profile styles. We bring samples to the estimate so you can see them against your siding and trim. If your HOA has specific color requirements, send me the guidelines and I'll confirm the match before we order materials.
