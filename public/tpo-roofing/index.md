# TPO Roofing Solutions

Energy-efficient, durable thermoplastic membrane roofing for commercial properties and flat residential roofs.

## What is TPO Roofing?

TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) is a single-ply reflective roofing membrane made from polypropylene and ethylene-propylene rubber. It has become the fastest growing commercial roofing system.

## Benefits of TPO Roofing

- **Energy Efficient** - White membrane reflects up to 85% of solar heat, reducing cooling costs by 20-30%.
- **Durable & Long-Lasting** - Resistant to UV, punctures, tears, and extreme weather. Lasts 20-30 years.
- **Environmentally Friendly** - No chlorine, recyclable, helps meet green building certifications.
- **Low Maintenance** - Smooth surface resists dirt and mold. Easy to clean.
- **Cost-Effective** - Lower installation cost than many comparable systems.
- **Versatile** - Mechanically fastened, fully adhered, or ballasted installation.

## Installation Process

1. **Inspection & Preparation** - Assess existing structure, repair deck damage, ensure proper drainage.
2. **Insulation Installation** - High-R-value insulation boards for energy efficiency.
3. **Membrane Application** - TPO sheets rolled out, overlapped, and heat-welded for watertight seal.
4. **Detail Work & Inspection** - Seal all penetrations, flashings, and edges. Seam testing for complete waterproofing.
