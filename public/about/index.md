# About Stark Roofing & Renovation

GAF Certified. Family-Owned. Puget Sound.

## Meet Brenda Scarllet, Owner

Stark Roofing & Renovation is a GAF Certified contractor with decades of combined experience and 2,000+ roofs installed across the Greater Seattle area. Brenda Scarllet owns and runs the company alongside a seasoned sales and production team.

**Customer Satisfaction** - We are not finished until you are completely happy with the results.
