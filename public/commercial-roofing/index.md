## Commercial Roofing Systems

- **TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin)** - Energy-efficient white membrane, reflects up to 85% of solar heat. 20-30 year lifespan.
- **EPDM (Rubber Roofing)** - Durable, flexible, cost-effective for large flat roofs.
- **PVC Membrane** - Chemical-resistant, long-lasting. Ideal for restaurants and industrial.
- **Metal Standing Seam** - Premium option for commercial properties. 50+ year lifespan.
