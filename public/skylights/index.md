# Skylight Installation

Bring natural light into your home with professional VELUX skylight installation and expert flashing.

## VELUX Skylights We Install

### Solar Powered Fresh Air (VELUX VSS) - Most Popular
- Opens and closes by remote, no wiring needed
- Built-in rain sensor closes it for you
- Solar panel charges silently on the roof
- Best for: bathrooms, kitchens, vaulted ceilings

### Electric Fresh Air (VELUX VSE)
- Hardwired, controlled by wall switch or remote
- Built-in rain sensor
- Pairs with smart home systems
- Best for: new construction or remodels with accessible wiring

### Manual Fresh Air (VELUX VS)
- Hand-cranked operation
- Most affordable venting option
- No electronics, nothing to fail
- Best for: lower-pitched rooms within easy reach

### Fixed Skylight (VELUX FCM / FS)
- Pure light, maximum energy efficiency
- Lowest cost, simplest install
- Deck-mount or curb-mount options
- Best for: stairwells, hallways, closets

### SUN TUNNEL Tubular (VELUX TGR / TMR)
- Pipes daylight through a reflective tube
- Fits where a full skylight will not
- Looks like a recessed light from below
- Best for: small bathrooms, hallways, laundry rooms

## Benefits of Skylights

- Up to 3x more light than vertical windows of the same size
- Reduces need for artificial lighting
- Improves mood and well-being
- Better ventilation with vented models
- Creates a feeling of more space
