# Metal Roofing

Premium standing seam metal roofing for homes and businesses across the Puget Sound.

## Why Metal Roofing?

- **50+ year lifespan** - Outlasts asphalt shingles by decades
- **Moss-proof** - Metal does not support moss growth, a major advantage in the Pacific Northwest
- **Handles heavy rain** - Sheds water immediately with no absorption
- **Wind resistant** - Standing seam panels interlock for maximum wind resistance
- **Energy efficient** - Reflects solar heat, reducing cooling costs
- **Low maintenance** - No cleaning, no moss treatment, minimal upkeep

## Best For

- Waterfront and wind-exposed properties
- Heavily forested lots where moss is a constant problem
- Homeowners who want the lowest lifetime cost
- Modern and contemporary home designs
- Properties in Snoqualmie, North Bend, and higher elevations with snow
