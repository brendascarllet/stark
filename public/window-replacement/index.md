# Window Replacement

Drafty, foggy, 30 years old?

## Why Replace Your Windows

- **$101-$583/yr energy savings** (Energy Star figure for replacing single-pane)
- **$600 federal tax credit** for Energy Star Most Efficient windows (U-factor 0.22 or below) through 2032
- **50% less outside noise** with dual/triple-pane laminated glass
- **No more cold spots or condensation** on interior glass
- **Bedroom egress code compliance** (WA State requires 5.7 sq ft clear opening)
