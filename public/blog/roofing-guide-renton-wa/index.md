# Renton, WA Roofing Guide: Affordable Solutions for Every Neighborhood

*From the Highlands to Benson Hill, Renton homeowners need roofs built for PNW weather. Learn about costs, materials, and maintenance for Renton homes.*

## Renton Roofing: Quality Protection at Every Price Point

Renton offers some of the best home values in the greater Seattle area, with diverse neighborhoods ranging from the established Renton Highlands to growing communities like Benson Hill and Kennydale. No matter where you are in Renton, your roof needs to handle Washington's demanding weather.

### Renton Weather and Your Roof

Renton sits at the south end of Lake Washington, giving it a climate profile similar to Bellevue and Kirkland:

- **37+ inches of rain** annually with most falling October through May
- **Wind events** — especially in neighborhoods with lake or valley exposure
- **Moderate moss growth** — less extreme than heavily forested areas but still common
- **Occasional heavy snow** in elevated areas like Benson Hill

### Renton Neighborhood Roofing Needs

**Renton Highlands** — Older homes with many original roofs past their lifespan. Many homeowners here are overdue for replacement. Great opportunity to upgrade to modern architectural shingles.

**Kennydale** — Waterfront proximity means extra moisture. Wind-rated shingles recommended.

**Benson Hill** — Higher elevation means more weather exposure. Strong underlayment and quality flashing are essential.

**Downtown / The Landing** — Mix of newer construction and renovated homes. Roofs in good condition typically need only maintenance.
