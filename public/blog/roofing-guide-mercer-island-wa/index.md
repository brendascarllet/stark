## Mercer Island Roofing: Protecting Washington's Most Valuable Homes

Mercer Island is one of the most prestigious communities in the Pacific Northwest, with median home values well above the regional average. When your home represents a significant investment, your roof needs to match — in both quality and aesthetics.

### Why Mercer Island Roofing Requires Extra Care

Mercer Island's unique island location creates specific roofing considerations:

- **Complete water exposure** — surrounded by Lake Washington, humidity is higher than mainland neighborhoods
- **Wind from every direction** — no windbreak from surrounding terrain
- **Mature tree canopy** — established neighborhoods have large trees that drop debris and create shade
- **Aesthetic standards** — Mercer Island homeowners expect roofing that enhances curb appeal, not just protects
