## Western Washington Storm Threats

Western Washington does not get hail. The real threats here are:

- Windstorms with 60+ MPH gusts
- Falling trees and large limbs
- Atmospheric rivers dumping 2-4 inches of rain in 24 hours
- Ice dams in higher elevations
