# Professional Roof Cleaning Services

Restore your roof's appearance and extend its lifespan. Remove harmful moss, algae, and debris.

## Why Roof Cleaning Matters in the Pacific Northwest

Seattle's wet climate and tree coverage create ideal conditions for moss and algae growth. Left untreated, moss can:

- Shorten your roof's lifespan by 5-10 years
- Lift shingles and create leak pathways
- Cause structural rot in the roof deck
- Void manufacturer warranties

## Our Cleaning Services

- **Soft-wash moss removal** - Gentle, effective, safe for your shingles
- **Algae treatment** - Kills algae at the root
- **Debris removal** - Pine needles, leaves, branches cleared from roof and valleys
- **Zinc strip installation** - Long-term moss prevention along the ridge line
- **Gutter cleaning** - Included with roof cleaning service

## Recommended Schedule

- Most Puget Sound homes: every 2-3 years
- Heavily forested properties (Woodinville, Maple Valley, North Bend): every 1-2 years
